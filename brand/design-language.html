Brand & Product Design System

Calm, trustworthy, and unmistakably Mediterranean.

Kluch helps foreigners live in Montenegro. The design language carries that promise: a steady navy you can trust, a warm sand that feels like home, and a sea-green that points to the coast and the mountains. Geometric, generous, and quiet.

Principles

Four ideas behind every screen

🔑

The key, literally

"Kluch" means key. The keyhole in the mark is the throughline — we open doors to a new home and a new life.

🛡️

Trust first

People hand us their rent and their problems. Navy, clean structure, and no clutter signal a steady operator.

⛰️

Sense of place

Sand and sea-green are Montenegro — the Adriatic and the mountains. Warm, never cold or corporate.

◻️

Geometric calm

Bold shapes, soft rounded corners, lots of breathing room. Simple enough to feel effortless in any language.

The mark

One letter, three meanings

The K — structure & trust

A bold, confident letterform in brand navy. It anchors everything and reads instantly at app-icon size.

The keyhole — access

Carved from the negative space of the K. The promise of Kluch: the key to getting in and getting settled.

The mountain — Montenegro

A single sea-green peak grounds the mark in place. It's the only spot of accent colour, so it always draws the eye.

Clear space: keep at least one keyhole-width of padding around the mark. Minimum size 24px. Never recolour the K or stretch the icon.

Color

A warm, grounded palette

Navy leads. Sea-green accents — used sparingly. Sand and paper carry the surfaces so the product feels warm, not clinical.
