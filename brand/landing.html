Why Kluch

Moving to Montenegro is hard. Living here shouldn't be.

Foreigners and landlords don't speak the same language — literally. Rent, utility bills, a broken boiler, a residence permit: every little thing becomes a phone-call marathon in a language you don't speak. Kluch sits in the middle and just handles it.

How it works

Three steps to settled

1

Onboard once

Link your home with a code from your landlord or agency. Pick your language — English, Russian, or Montenegrin.

2

We handle the rest

Rent reminders, bills, repairs, inspections, paperwork. Message us anytime and we reply in your language.

3

Everything in one place

No more chasing landlords or agencies. Your home, your documents, your services — one calm app.
