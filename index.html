The Problem

Foreigners and landlords
don't speak the same language.

For landlords

  • Calls at midnight about a leaking tap.
  • Foreign tenants paying late, in cash, with no audit trail.
  • Traditional agencies take 15–25% of rent.

For occupants

  • No one to call who speaks English (or Russian, German, Turkish).
  • Bills, residency, taxes — fragmented across dozens of small offices.
  • No trusted operator for the rest of life logistics.
The Solution

One Telegram bot.
One trusted operator.

Kluch sits between every landlord and every occupant. Onboard once — we handle the rest. Rent, bills, inspections, maintenance, translation, and eventually every other service a foreigner needs in Montenegro.

Landlord side
List once. Receive a monthly statement and a rent payout. Never touch it again.
Occupant side
Pay rent and bills in your language. Report issues with a photo. Discover perks across Montenegro.
The Brand

Kluch.

Ključ / ключ — "key" in the languages of 80% of our customers. In English it reads as clutch: dependable, comes through when it matters.

Every product line maps onto the metaphor: Rent Key. Residency Key. Tax Key. Company Key.

Domain: kluch.me  ·  .me is Montenegro's country code. The brand is the URL.

Competition

In Montenegro: no one.

International landlord SaaS doesn't translate, doesn't dispatch handymen, has no MNE presence. Local agencies do it manually. The lane is empty.

The playbook is proven elsewhere

EmerhubBali / Indonesia / Vietnam / PhilippinesForeigner services — visas, company formation, accommodation, banking, EOR. Multi-million revenue.

Our twist: we start with recurring rental relationships and layer services on top. They started with transactions. Our wedge is stickier.

Funding Strategy

Grant-first. Bootstrap if it misses.

Primary target

Innovation Fund of Montenegro

Early-Stage Startup Support program.

  • Up to €30,000 grant · covers 90% of project costs
  • Eligibility: MNE d.o.o. < 2 years, founder-owned, MVP/pilot
  • No user-count threshold. 5 paying landlords = strong applicant

Stackable

  • Innovation Vouchers — up to €8k for R&D partnership
  • Investment & Development Fund (IRF) — working-capital loans later
  • EU IPA / Western Balkans funds — revisit at €500k ARR

Bootstrap path

Founders unpaid, ops lead ~€800/mo, burn ~€20–30k/yr — covered by early subscription + cofounder's existing book.
