The Problem

Agencies are invisible.
Foreigners are lost.

For agencies

  • Broken, dated, mono-lingual websites — or none at all.
  • Listings scattered across classifieds and Facebook.
  • No way to reach the foreigners actually searching.

For foreigners

  • No single place to search rentals across agencies.
  • Then residency, company, tax — a fresh fragmented hunt each time.
  • Language and trust barriers on top of everything.

A free website for agencies.
One marketplace for foreigners.

Kluche gives every rental agency a polished, multilingual website for free — their brand, their listings. Those listings flow into one marketplace foreigners can actually search. And it's all reached through a single Kluche account.

Competitive landscape

No one aggregates
Montenegrin agencies.

  • International landlord SaaS is US-centric — no local presence, no translation, no marketplace.
  • Local agencies operate manually — none wrapped in software or aggregated.
  • The "operating system for foreigners" model is proven elsewhere (e.g. Emerhub in SE Asia, relocation firms in Iberia). Kluche's twist: start from the recurring rental relationship, then layer the services on one account.
Ownership & legal

Two companies,
one clean line.

The cofounder's rental agency stays 100% his. Kluche is a new d.o.o. owned by the two founders; the agency becomes Kluche's first customer at arm's length.

  • Shareholders' Agreement: vesting, deadlock, reserved matters.
  • Explicit scope line between the agency and Kluche.
  • IP assignment of code & brand to the d.o.o.
Funding

A live pilot beats
a pitch deck.

Primary target: the Innovation Fund of Montenegro early-stage support — for a registered, founder-owned startup with a working product. Real agencies on live sites with aggregated listings is concrete evidence.

Secondary: innovation vouchers for the AI/translation R&D; development-fund instruments later. Bootstrappable in the meantime on featured placements and the cofounder's book.

Grant amounts & deadlines: confirm against the current open call.
